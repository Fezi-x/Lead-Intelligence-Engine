# Lead Qualification Criteria

## Geography
- Yangon
- Mandalay
- Bangkok

## Industry Types
- Medical (Clinic, Dentist, Skincare, etc)
- Education (School, Tuition, Private College, Private School, Kindergarden, Ed Consultent, etc)
- F&B (Restaurant, Cafe, etc)
- Construction
- Hotel
- Agencies (Real Estate, Digital Marketing, etc)
- Personal Brands

## Industry-Specific Exclusions (Internal Logic)
- **Digital Marketing Agencies**: Do NOT sell "Marketing Services" or "Marketing Packages". Focus on "Technology Services" (Website/Custom Dev) or "Add-ons" only if their own presence is weak.
- **Software/IT Companies**: Do NOT sell "Technology Services" (Website/Custom Dev). Focus on "Marketing Services" or "Strategy" only.

## Business Size Indicators
- Estimated team: 10–50
- Revenue proxy: MMK ~35M range
- Growth-stage (2–7 years)

# Digital Maturity Evaluation Framework

## A. Visibility Layer

### Signals to Detect
- Posting frequency
- Engagement ratio
- Paid ads presence
- Website existence

### Risk Flags
- High engagement but no visible CTA
- Boosted posts without structured funnel

## Website Evaluation

Check:
- Lead capture form exists
- Booking system present
- CTA clarity
- Contact info visibility
- Mobile optimization
- Pixel / tracking scripts detected

Flag:
- Brochure-only website
- No clear action path

## Measurement Signals

Detect:
- Meta Pixel
- GA4
- Tag Manager
- Conversion event triggers

Flag:
- No tracking scripts
- No visible funnel tracking

## Operational Maturity

Signals:
- Automated booking confirmation
- Structured FAQ system
- Chatbot presence
- Email capture

Risk:
- Messenger-only communication
- Manual phone-based booking

## Asset Ownership Check

Check:
- Owned domain
- Email list capture
- SEO pages
- Google Business presence

Flag:
- Facebook-only business
