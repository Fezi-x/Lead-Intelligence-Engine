# Sales Strategy: Efficiency First
## Conversation Angle Prediction Framework

Purpose:
After evaluating a business profile, the system generates a predicted conversation entry angle.

This is not outreach copy.
This is strategic framing intelligence.

---

# 1. Core Philosophy

We do not assume problems.
We infer structural inefficiencies based on observable signals.

We do not sell services.
We identify leverage points.

The output must answer:
"If we were to start a strategic discussion, what is the most logical entry point?"

---

# 2. Angle Selection Hierarchy

Angles are selected based on highest structural risk.

Priority order:

1. Revenue Visibility Gap
2. Conversion Infrastructure Weakness
3. Operational Bottleneck
4. Platform Dependency Risk
5. Underutilized Attention
6. Competitive Positioning Gap

---

# 3. Angle Definitions

---

## A. Revenue Visibility Gap Angle

Trigger Conditions:
- No analytics detected
- No conversion tracking
- Ads visible but no measurable funnel
- No data signals

Predicted Conversation Direction:
Discussion about measurable growth and revenue clarity.

Core Framing:
"Your growth decisions may not be tied to real performance data."

Strategic Depth:
Focus on CAC, LTV, tracking architecture, decision confidence.

---

## B. Conversion Infrastructure Angle

Trigger Conditions:
- Website exists
- No lead capture
- Weak CTA
- No booking automation
- High engagement but low structural conversion path

Predicted Conversation Direction:
Discussion about converting existing traffic into measurable revenue.

Core Framing:
"Attention exists, but the system converting it into revenue may be incomplete."

Strategic Depth:
Landing structure, funnel design, CTA optimization.

---

## C. Operational Bottleneck Angle

Trigger Conditions:
- Messenger-only communication
- Manual booking
- No automation signals
- No structured follow-up

Predicted Conversation Direction:
Discussion about operational scalability and efficiency.

Core Framing:
"Growth may be constrained by manual processes."

Strategic Depth:
Automation, CRM integration, response time optimization.

---

## D. Platform Dependency Angle

Trigger Conditions:
- Facebook-only presence
- No owned domain
- No email capture
- No SEO footprint

Predicted Conversation Direction:
Discussion about digital asset ownership and risk mitigation.

Core Framing:
"Current growth relies on rented platforms."

Strategic Depth:
Digital equity, asset control, long-term stability.

---

## E. Underutilized Attention Angle

Trigger Conditions:
- Strong engagement metrics
- Active posting
- Weak conversion infrastructure
- No structured funnel

Predicted Conversation Direction:
Discussion about amplifying existing attention into predictable acquisition.

Core Framing:
"There is visible demand, but it is not fully captured."

Strategic Depth:
Funnel layering, retargeting, segmentation.

---

## F. Competitive Positioning Angle

Trigger Conditions:
- Highly saturated industry
- Generic messaging
- No differentiation visible
- Copy-based positioning

Predicted Conversation Direction:
Discussion about structural differentiation and defensibility.

Core Framing:
"Increased competition requires systematic positioning."

Strategic Depth:
Offer clarity, category positioning, strategic branding.

---

# 4. Output Structure for Angle Prediction

After evaluation, system must generate:

## Business Snapshot
- Industry
- Location
- Digital maturity level

## Primary Structural Gap
Single highest impact inefficiency.

## Predicted Conversation Angle
One of the defined angles above.

## Strategic Entry Point
One sentence describing how discussion should begin.

## Opportunity Leverage Statement
One sentence describing what unlocks growth.

---

# 5. Language Rules for Generated Angle

Tone:
- Analytical
- Neutral
- Structured
- Non-assumptive

Avoid:
- Emotional triggers
- Pressure language
- Sales phrasing
- Hype framing

Use:
- Infrastructure
- Visibility
- Optimization
- Leverage
- Scalability
- Architecture

---

# 6. What This Is NOT

- Not a sales script
- Not a pitch
- Not a marketing proposal
- Not persuasion copy

This is pre-conversation strategic intelligence.

---

# 7. Foundational Principle

Every SME problem visible externally
is a surface signal of a structural system gap.

The goal of angle prediction
is to identify the most economically meaningful system gap.
